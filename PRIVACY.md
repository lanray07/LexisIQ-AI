# LexisIQ AI Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 29, 2026

LexisIQ AI is designed as an informational legal productivity app. The current app build stores user-created profile, matter, research, document, voice-note, contract-review and chronology data locally on the user's device using Apple platform storage.

The developer does not collect personal data from the app in the current build. Mock AI is enabled by default. If a future version connects to a remote AI backend, document-processing service, analytics service, account system or support system, this policy and the App Store privacy declaration should be updated before release.

Speech recognition, subscriptions, payments, crash diagnostics and device services may be handled by Apple according to Apple's terms and privacy policies.

LexisIQ AI is an informational productivity tool only. It does not provide legal advice, legal representation or guarantees of legal outcomes. AI-generated output may contain errors and should be reviewed by a qualified lawyer with jurisdiction-specific verification.

For support, contact the developer through the GitHub repository:
https://github.com/lanray07/LexisIQ-AI/issues
